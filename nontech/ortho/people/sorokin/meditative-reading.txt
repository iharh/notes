        - (a esli, k primeru, chitaem v bibleyskoy gruppe i nechego skazat po povodu texta, to chustvuem nekuyu nepolnotsennost)

    Nekotorye psalmy specialno prednaznacheny dlya obucheniya navyku podobnogo chteniya

    Chto zdes proishodit pri meditativnom chtenii?
        - chelovek dolzhen prebyvat postoyanno v sostoyanii slushaniya, vospriyatiya
        - chem govoreniya, producirovaniya intentsionalno chego-to vo vne
        - smotrite, sam text vedyot cheloveka k activizatsii ego intentsii
        - a zatem - k deactivizacii

    To est, chitaya text meditativno,
        - chelovek dolzhen perezhit opyt activnoy intentsii (obychnoy molitvy)
        - i passivnoy (vnutrenney molitvy, sozertsatelnoy)

    Text provodit cheloveka cherez raznye (k razmym) formy i sposoby molitvy
        - i kazhdomu sposobu on pridayot svoyu slovesnuyu obolochku

    Esli nam nuzhna sozertsatelnaya molitva
        - to na urovne passivnoy intentsii my poluchaem obraz
        - kotoryi derzhit vsyu nashu molitvu kak f nekoem futlyare
        - on stanovitsya toy vnutrenney comnatoy, iz kotoroy my mozhem molitsya
        - i ne bylo by u nas passivnoy intentsii, etot obraz ne poyavilya by
        - a my by srazu stali by razmyshlyat i voobrazhat sebe goru, hram
        - stali by razmyshlyat o tom, chto znachit tam byt
        - poyavilas by kusha emotsiy i vsyo poshlo by na smarku
        - tolko s passivnoy/slushayuschey intentsiey mozhno vosprinyat etot 5 stih
        - kak obolochku dlya vnutrenney molitvy

    I molyashiysha dolzhen practicovat passivnoe vnimanie

        - s drugoy storony, k ney on idyot cherez molitvu obychnuyu
        - no ne bud passivnoy vosprinimayuschey intentsii - ne bylo by i vnutrenney molitvy
        - a eti stihi ili byli by nezamecheny ili porodili by ...

        - cheloveku na opyte pokazano, kak mozhno pereyti chitaya text ot chistogo slushaniya
        - k chemu-to bolee activnogo, esli sam text etogo trebuet
